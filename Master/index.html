About Us

Created by a group of young developers inspired to change the way people spend their gaps of time getting to know the city they are in.

Beginning exclusively with Detroit, home to 3 of the 4 creators, D-Tour provides a walking itinerary guide for people with short gaps of time to fill. Whether it be from a layover flight in the city, or employees working in the city on their lunch break.
